privacy & data usage

omi search (third-party app)

omi search is an independent community integration and is not an official product from omi or omi labs. use it at your own discretion and review this policy carefully before enabling the webhook.

the integration receives transcript segments from your omi device, buffers them temporarily to detect useful topics, and only forwards minimal excerpts to the brave search api when a query is triggered. transcripts are not permanently stored unless they are attached to a search record in your supabase project.

  • conversation snippets tied to search results are saved under your uid inside the search_queries table.
  • you can delete individual searches from the dashboard or remove the app to stop further processing.
  • cadence controls let you throttle how often new searches run.
  • only the generated search text is sent to brave‚Äîno full transcripts or personal identifiers are shared.
